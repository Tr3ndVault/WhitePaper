Roadmap

Phase 1: Community Establishment

  • Define community goals and objectives
  • Create a private online platform for the community
  • Invite and onboard initial members
  • Establish community guidelines and rules

Phase 2: Content Sharing

  • Initiate discussions on investment strategies
  • Share educational content related to investments
  • Encourage members to share their insights

Phase 3: Investment Calls

  • Introduce a structured format for investment calls
  • Coordinate regular meetings or webinars
  • Facilitate collaborative decision-making on investment opportunities
